# Sound Effects (SFX)

Place your sound effect files here.

**Supported formats:** WAV, MP3, M4A, OGG

**Example SFX for horror videos:**
- `thunder.wav` - Thunder clap
- `creak.wav` - Door creak
- `wind.wav` - Howling wind
- `heartbeat.wav` - Heartbeat sound
- `whisper.wav` - Creepy whisper
- `scream.wav` - Scream
- `footsteps.wav` - Footsteps on wood
- `breathing.wav` - Heavy breathing
- `door_slam.wav` - Door slamming

**Tips:**
- Keep SFX files short (1-5 seconds typically)
- Use high-quality samples (44.1kHz or 48kHz)
- Adjust volume in config.json rather than pre-processing
- Layer multiple SFX for richer sound design

**Free SFX sources:**
- Freesound.org
- Zapsplat.com
- BBC Sound Effects Library
- YouTube Audio Library
